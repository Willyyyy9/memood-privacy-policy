Privacy Policy for MeMood

Effective Date: September 07, 2025

MeMood (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is owned and operated by AccessibilityX (“the Company”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application (“App”).  

---

1. Information We Collect
When you use MeMood, we may collect the following types of information:  

- Mood Entries & Notes: The moods you log, journal entries, and reflections.  
- Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as frequency of use, features accessed, and session duration.  
- Optional Personal Data: If you choose to provide it, such as your name, email address, or profile picture (e.g., for backups or syncing across devices).  
- Device Information: Basic details like device type, operating system, app version, and crash reports.  

---

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:  

- Help you track and reflect on your moods over time.  
- Personalize your experience and provide insights.  
- Improve the performance and reliability of MeMood.  
- Respond to support requests and communicate with you.  
- Maintain app security and prevent misuse.  

---

3. Data Storage & Security
- Your data is stored securely on your device by default.  
- If cloud backup or account features are enabled, your data may also be stored on secure servers.  
- We use encryption and industry-standard security measures to protect your information.  
- However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.  

---

4. Sharing of Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data is only shared in the following cases:  

- With Your Consent: If you choose to export or share your mood data.  
- Service Providers: Trusted third parties that help us operate the App (e.g., analytics, crash reporting).  
- Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.  

---

5. Your Rights
Depending on your region, you may have the right to:  

- Access, update, or delete your personal data.  
- Export your mood data.  
- Withdraw consent for optional data collection.  
- Contact us regarding concerns about your privacy.  

To exercise these rights, please email us at support@memoodapp.com.  

---

6. Children’s Privacy
MeMood is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us immediately.  

---

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the “Effective Date” above. Significant updates will be communicated within the App or through email (if provided).
